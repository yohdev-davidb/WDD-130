Site Plan for BrookeBound Rapids

Dave Brooke

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my rafting business's website is to enhance my customer's experience by providing easy online booking, detailed information about my trips and services, and engaging content that reflects our commitment to safety, and adventure. My goal is to build revenue through increased bookings and merchandise sales, while also creating a community of outdoor enthusiasts who share our passion for adventure.

Audience

My target audience for BrookeBound Rapids website includes adventure-seeking individuals, families looking for outdoor activities, corporate groups interested in team-building exercises, and educational groups aiming to learn about nature and river ecosystems. My customers are typically between the ages of 18-50, have a moderate to high income level, and enjoy outdoor recreational activities. They seek excitement, adventure, and unique experiences that bring them closer to nature. Scenario Questions and Answers Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on). Scenario: Sarah, a 28-year-old marketing professional, enjoys spending her weekends exploring outdoor activities with friends. She has a moderate to high income and is always on the lookout for new adventures to share on her social media. Answer: Sarah is part of my target audience as she represents young professionals with disposable income who seek thrilling experiences and enjoy sharing their adventures online. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Scenario: John and Emily, a couple in their early 40s with two children, are planning their summer vacation. They are looking for family-friendly outdoor activities that are safe and enjoyable for their kids. Answer: John and Emily expect to find detailed information about family-friendly rafting trips, including safety measures, age-appropriate activities, and booking options. They want to know that their children will be safe and have a great time. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Scenario: Mike, a 35-year-old corporate manager, is organizing a team-building event for his team of 20 employees. He needs to gather information quickly while on the go, using his smartphone during breaks at work. Answer: Mike will primarily access the site via his mobile device. The website should be mobile-friendly, with easy navigation and quick access to booking options, trip details, and contact information.
